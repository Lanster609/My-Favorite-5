About

My hobby is playing soccer in my free time. I play on a team but it is not very professional and we only have minor games. Usually I play the position right back, but I play others too. I play with my friend and have only been playing since I was 9 years old. We have practices every Tuesday and games every Saturday. My games typically last about an hour and so do my practices. We play in increments of seasons. One in the fall, one in the spring. We have about ten games every season and about 8 practices. I play with boys and girls around my age on a team. Our games are mostly easy-going, but sometimes people trip or knock into someone and get hurt. Playing soccer gives me something to do and makes me happy, and when I'm not playing I miss it a lot.


My Positions

When I am playing soccer, my main position is right back, but it is possible for me to switch positions in a game. I can play all of the defensive positions if I need to. I can also play a defensive mid, which is where you are still playing defense, but you can move all the way up the feild if you have to. These are my most common positions but occasionally I'll move into the middle, but thats as far as I go.
